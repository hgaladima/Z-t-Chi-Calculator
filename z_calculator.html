Area Under the Normal Curve Calculator

Calculate probabilities and areas under the standard normal distribution

About Normal Distribution: The normal distribution is a symmetric, bell-shaped curve where the area under the curve represents probability. Z-scores indicate how many standard deviations an observation is from the mean.

Z-score to Probability Calculator

Instructions:

  • Enter a Z-score to find the corresponding area under the curve (AUC)
  • The result shows the probability P(Z ≤ z) - area to the left of Z
  • Z-scores can be positive or negative
Example: 1.96, -1.645, etc.
